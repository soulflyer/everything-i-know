Flipping cube edges. 1 edge can't be flipped on a 3x3. 

* 1 edge
r  U2 x r U2 r  U2
r' U2  l  U2 r' U2
r  U2  r' U2 r'

For 5x5 this is almost the same. Note the extra layer that is rotated near the end of verse 2
Note that it doesn't flip the whole row, only the outer parts. Flipping the centre one is not possible, or useful.

r' U2  l  U2  3R' U2
