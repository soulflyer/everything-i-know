#+begin_src clojure
(defn map-all [f & colls]
  (lazy-seq
   (when (some seq colls)
     (cons (apply f (map first colls))
           (apply map-all f (map rest colls))))))
#+end_src

Keeps going until all collections are empty rather than stopping when the first is empty like map does.
