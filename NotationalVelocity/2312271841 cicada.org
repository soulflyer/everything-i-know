To get the session ID:
#+begin_src shell
curl -X POST localhost:3333/api/v1/login -i -H 'Content-type: application/json' -d '{"login": "owner", "password": "not_nil"}'
#+end_src

Open devtools and replace the session Id under app on http://localhost:3333/api/v1/swagger/swagger-ui
