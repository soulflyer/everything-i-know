#+TITLE: emacs yasnippets
# TAGS #emacs #yasnippets

** show all a vailable snippets:
M-x (yas-describe-tables)

** insert a snippet, promts for which. Can use tab completion to select
M-x (yas-insert-snippet)

Snippets can also be inserted by typing the trigger key and hitting tab

** Editing a snippet
(yas-visit-snippet-file)  C-c & C-v
C-c C-c to save edited snippet
