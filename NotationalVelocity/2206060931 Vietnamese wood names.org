* Ebony, gỗ mun
** gỗ mun
*Diospyros mun*, https://www.wood-database.com/mun-ebony/

** gỗ mun sừng
Might be just another name for *Diospyros mun*

** gỗ mun hoa
Probably black and white ebony:
*Diospyros malabarica* https://www.wood-database.com/black-and-white-ebony/
Check the density. Black and White Ebony is comparitively light and soft (Still harder than oak)
Price around 140k per kg https://www.facebook.com/ra.hu.165
** Other possibilities
These species are from this part of the world and may also be locally available:
*Diospyros ebenum* Ceylon ebony, the "original"
*Diospyros celebica* Macassar ebony
