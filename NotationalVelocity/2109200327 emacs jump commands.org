#+TITLE: emacs jump commands
# Tags #emacs

*M-x jump-char-forward*
bound to *M-j*
Highlights all occurences of the choosen character. Press the same char to move forward, or:

;        next match forwars
,        next match backwards
C-c C-c  invokes ace-jump-mode

*M-x avy-goto-char-in-line*
bound to *C-c C-j*
highlights all occurences of the given char on the current line, selected by choosing the char that they are temporarily replaced by.
