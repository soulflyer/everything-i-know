#+TITLE: Dix Hallpike diagnosis for BPPV
# Tags #health
Dizziness due to

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wgWOmuB1VFY

nystagmus - eye twitching
 * torsional - rotating
 * horizontal - side to side
 * vertical - up and down
Head turned to 45°, lie flat back, shoulders raised by a pillow so head is sloping back 20° to 30°.
Look for nystagmus. Repeat on the other side. Repeat several times:
 * torsional nystagmus - Epley manoeuver
 * horizontal nystagmus - Lempert Manoeuver
 * vertical nystagmus - Deep head hanging manoeuver
