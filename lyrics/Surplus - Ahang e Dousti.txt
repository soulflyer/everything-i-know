An Autumn Night In My Land
Brightly illuminates the stars
A breeze enters from the port
And the dust dances

The passage of time is not felt for several hundred years
Months pass and few of us are left
And those we knew were gone

They are gone from us
All their laughter
Their glory and youth
Such a friendship will never be forgotten
Blood sanctified by love
They will return
To sprout among us

Friendship beyond words
Silent and gray
From the blood and pain of those days
A fire will burn among us
A friendship in whose name we will move forward
For all the comrades who fell on their swords
A precious gift to remember

They will return
To sprout among us
