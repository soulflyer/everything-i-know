** Scales and positions

1st position is great for the major scale, 2nd for blues scales, and 3rd for minor scales. Here are some useful scales to start with:

1st position major scale: 4 -4  5  -5   6   -6   -7   7

2nd position blues scale: -2   -3/   4  -4/   -4    -5   6

3rd position minor scale: -1     2    -2//  -2   -3//   -3/   4     -4

3rd position blues scale: -1  -2//  -2   -3//  -3/   4   -4
