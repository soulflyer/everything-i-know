** Start Blowing [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MyUkliJ9LM8][9 ways to start blowing]]
*** Phuha
- Find the note, then push out with the lower lip until the sound stops and there is only breath noise
- Close lips and inhale
- Start blowing and bring the lower lip back
*** Huha
- as *Pha* but don't close the lips
*** Hahu
- as *Huha* but open the lips then start to close them again as you start the note
