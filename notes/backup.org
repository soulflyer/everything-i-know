* Backing up using rsync

rsync with the *-n* option will do a dry run:
*--delete* will remove anything from the destination that is not present on the source. Use *--backup-dir=/tmp/backup* to preserve these.

This one will copy any master copies of photos that have been added to mimi onto the external disk blue
#+begin_src shell
rsync -vaibn /Volumes/OfflinePhotos/ /Volumes/Blue/OfflinePhotos
#+end_src
