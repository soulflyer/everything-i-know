* Dorithricin
Sore throat, swallowing difficulties. Claims to fight bacteria and viruses. Anaesthetic and anti-bacterial.

* Cefixim
Antibiotic for strep throat, otitis media, urine infections etc
Cefixim is the German name. More usually called Cefixime.

* Myltuss
Dextromethorphan. A cough suppressant. Side effects include drowsiness and hallucinations (high doses). Known to interact with other drugs and grapefruit.

* Kacerin
Cetirizine. Antihistamine. Can cause drowsiness.

* Theraline
Alimemazine. Antihistamine, sedative, hypnotic and anti-emetic.

* Glotadol
Paracetamol and caffeine.

* Eugica
Essential oils incl peppermint, ginger etc.
